My Book

13 Years in The Dark World is the gripping true story of Vukosi Sono Khiba’s journey through spiritual captivity and ultimate deliverance. Trapped in the dark world for over a
decade, she unknowingly entered demonic covenants that manipulated her destiny. From spiritual initiations to assignments against the church, she unveils the hidden operations of
darkness. However, through the power of Jesus Christ, she finds redemption, breaks free from bondage and reclaims her life. This book is both a warning and a guide, revealing
the reality of spiritual warfare and the authority believers have in Christ. A testimony of transformation, it equips readers to discern, fight and walk in the freedom that only God
can give.
